Our Technology

Software Driven.
Hardware Enabled.

STRYD combines advanced biosensing hardware with intelligent software to deliver real-time muscle activation and gait analytics — directly to patients recovering at home.

Real-Time Muscle Activation Monitoring

Embedded EMG sensors capture electrical signals from muscle contractions, providing real-time feedback on muscle engagement patterns during rehabilitation exercises.

At-Home Rehabilitation

Bringing clinical-grade monitoring out of the clinic and into patients' homes — enabling continuous, informed recovery without compromise.

Gait Analysis

IMU-based motion tracking captures detailed gait patterns, detecting asymmetries and tracking recovery progress over time.

Intelligent Software Platform

Cloud-connected analytics dashboard delivers actionable insights to patients and clinicians, enabling data-driven rehabilitation decisions.

Progress Tracking

Longitudinal data collection enables visualization of recovery milestones, compliance metrics, and outcome trends for optimized rehabilitation.
